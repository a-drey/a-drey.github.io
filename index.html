Hardware Chart

Hard Drive

A hard drive stores the data, software, and files on a device.

PCI Card

PCI/PCIe cards connect hardware to a computer.

RAM Card

RAM is the random access memory on a computer that only activates the data from the hard drive that is needed for a certain task.

Motherboard

A motherboard controls all of the hardware and connects it together so that it can perform certain tasks.

Power Supply

The power supply must supply the computer with power by transforming certain kinds of voltage.
